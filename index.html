BEFORE WE BUILD YOUR PORTFOLIO

How your portfolio
is constructed

①
Your answers drive the allocation
Your goal, time horizon, risk tolerance, income needs, liquidity, inflation concern, international preference and USD view are used to determine how much weight to assign to each asset class.
②
Lowest expense ratio ETF in each class
Once the asset class weights are set, the tool selects the single lowest expense ratio ETF available within each category from a universe of 130+ ETFs across issuers including Vanguard, BlackRock, Schwab, Fidelity and State Street.
③
Trimmed to your target position count
The engine may identify more categories than your target. The highest-conviction positions are kept and weights are renormalized so your portfolio always sums to 100%.
